About

This website is dedicated to football analysis, however, this website also promotes the donation to a Italian charity known as Missione Possibile.

Mission

Missione Possibile is a non-profit organization that aims to protect the fundamental human rights. Some of the rights that they protect are education, adequate medical care, and freedom of thought. The mission which Missione Possibile aims to move forward with is to bring hope and love; most of the projects aiming to reach this goal is based in Cambodia.

Children

Currently, Missione Possibile has schools that is open to students in Cambodia in elementary, middle, and high school. In total there are over 600 students attending these schools by the help of the Missione Possibile's school project. This projects aids the families and children that are not able to afford the money to send their children to schools, therefore, this organization is helpful to those in need.

Football

Football has become a gargantuan part of Missione Possibile's school project. A project called the Inter Campus began in 2011; this project involves the education and sports activities of 175 boys and girls. In this project, the students who attend Missione Possibile's school in the Roong Village, in Cambodia, is where the Inter Campus coaches come to the village twice a year and update them on new trainings.
