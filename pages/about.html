Mission

This website is dedicated to football analysis, however, this website also promotes the donation to a Italian charity known as Missione Possibile.


Missione Possibile is a non-profit organization that aims to protect the fundamental human rights. Some of the rights that they protect are education, adequate medical care, and freedom of thought. The mission which Missione Possibile aims to move forward with is to bring hope and love; most of the projects aiming to reach this goal is based in Cambodia.
